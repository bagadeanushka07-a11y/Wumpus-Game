📜 HOW TO PLAY – HUNT THE WUMPUS

🐗 Goal: Kill the Wumpus before it eats you! The beast hides somewhere in the 6x6 cave.

👃 Stench: If you are next to the Wumpus (up/down/left/right), you'll smell its foul stench → a warning appears.

🎮 Movement: Use the MOVEMENT CONTROLLER (4 buttons) OR keyboard W A S D.

🏹 Shooting: Use the SHOOTING CONTROLLER (4 direction buttons) OR ▲ ▼ ◀ ▶ arrow keys. You must be adjacent (next to) the Wumpus and shoot in its direction to kill it.

💀 Danger: Stepping on the Wumpus = you're eaten. Game over.

🏆 Victory: Shoot the Wumpus from an adjacent cell! You have unlimited arrows, but aim wisely.

✔️ Fully responsive | On-screen buttons + full keyboard support | Restart anytime
